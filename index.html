Get outside with UTCH

We organize hikes, paddles, camping trips, and other adventures for UT students and community members. Check the calendar for upcoming trips and RSVP to join.

View Upcoming Trips Suggest a Trip RSVP to a Trip
Weekly meeting: 7:00 PM in AMB 27 unless otherwise posted.

How to join a trip

Find a trip on the calendar, open the event details, then use the RSVP link (with the Trip ID). Officers use RSVPs to finalize the list and coordinate meet-up details.

Tip: Add the UTCH Trips calendar to your own calendar app so you always have it with you.

Have an idea?

Suggest a trip (and optionally volunteer to lead). We'll follow up and work with you to get it on the calendar.

Leading can be as simple as picking a trail and helping keep the group together.
